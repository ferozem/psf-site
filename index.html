What we do

About PSF in 3 simple steps

01

Our Mission

To provide a platform for all professionals to use their skills, time and creativity for development of self and society, to bring about a positive change in the society.

02

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leader in social change philanthropy and to mobilize philanthropic resources to promote unity, sense of coexistence and communal harmony.

03

Our Objectives

Sensitize professionals, regarding the purpose of their life, and to think beyond their routine professional and personal life.
